Shakib Al Hasan

Shakib Al Hasan is a Bangladeshi international cricketer who was consistently ICC's no.1 all rounder in all three formats of the game for a decade. He was ranked as the 2nd most valuable player of the century according to Wisden Cricketers' Almanack.
